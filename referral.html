About our referral programme

One of our most powerful mechanisms to attract great talent and likeminded clients is through referrals from our wonderful community. With this in mind, we’ve created a referral programme with great incentives, which means everyone can benefit.

If you refer a talented person to us and we hire them, or you refer someone to us who then decides to use our services, we’ll give you a cash reward*. Read more about how this works.

It doesn’t matter whether you refer a friend, cousin or colleague to us - we just want to meet great people who have digital skills we need, or who could benefit from the services we offer.

What you can earn

When you refer either digital talent or potential clients to us, here’s what you stand to earn as a referral fee:

Your placed candidate:

R10 000

If we successfully onboard your referred candidate at one of our clients and they pass the client’s probation period, you’ll get R10 000 in cash in your bank account as a thank you from us.

Referred candidate signing bonus:

R5 000

If the onboarded candidate you referred passes the client’s probation period, that candidate will receive a R5 000 signing bonus at the end of the probation period or 3 months after starting with us (whichever comes first).

New client AND new candidate:

R20 000

Should you refer a new client to us plus new talent, you’ll get R20 000 commission for both successful submissions once the candidate has passed the client’s probation period.

New successful division referral within current client:

R5 000

Should the new client fall within a current Strider client (for example a new division with a current client), you’ll earn commission of R5 000.

New client referral with successful candidate placed:

R10 000

If you refer a new client to us and we sign a contract with them that specifies we will place a Strider person at that company for 6 months or more, you’ll automatically earn R10 000 in cash.

New division within current client AND new candidate:

R15 000

If you refer a new candidate placed within a new division of a current client’s business, you’ll get a R15 000 payout after the three month probation period ends.
